About Me

My love affair with diner food began back when I was in college at Michigan State University. Once in a while I used to go out to diners around town with my friends. Chowing down fries, taking in the atmosphere, it got me thinking about writing a writing a coffee table book about cool diners. However, like most college student I lacked the funding for anything more than a cheeseburger, let alone to publish a book. So when I gained some new skills, I decided to revisit that idea in a digital format. So click around, read some articles, even submit a picture! Just don't forget to connect with us on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and Tumblr!
